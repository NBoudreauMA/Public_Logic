Turn Municipal Chaos into Digital Clarity

Municipal-First by Design. Public_Logic is not theory, and it is not vendor-driven. It is a municipal-first framework built from the lived reality of town halls, public meetings, and staff who make local government run.

VAULT connects your people, data, and systems into compliant, repeatable workflows — without replacing tools you already use.

Start a Conversation Explore VAULT

The Municipal Operating System

At the foundation is ARCHIVE: the standard base model and logic layer. Every other module inherits its standards for accountability, records, and continuity.

ARCHIVE

Accountability, Records, Compliance, History, Integrity, Governance, Evidence

The logic layer and standard base model. ARCHIVE ensures that every action, record, and decision is traceable, defensible, and continuous across transitions in staff and boards.

CLERK

Compliance, Licensing, Engagement, Records, Knowledge

Supports the Clerk's office in licensing, public records, statutory postings, and meeting notices. Protects transparency and preserves institutional knowledge.

APEX

Accounts Payable and Expenditures

Brings order to turnovers, invoices, and warrant preparation. Ensures boards receive timely, accurate packets and compliance with state requirements.

FISCAL

Fiscal Stewardship, Controls, Accountability, and Law

Adds the compliance framework around existing accounting systems. Covers receipts, reconciliations, transfers, and reporting for smoother audits and stronger oversight.

MEET

Meetings, Engagement, Exhibits, Transparency

Structures board and committee workflow. Ensures packets, exhibits, minutes, and follow-ups are complete and compliant.

LIC

Local Issuance and Compliance

Framework for Select Board licenses and local permits, including renewals and departmental sign-offs. Keeps routing clear and compliant.

INSPECT

Inspections, Notifications, Safety, Permits, Enforcement, Compliance, Tracking

Covers building, zoning, and site inspections. Tracks permits from intake to certificate of occupancy with accountability at every stage.

PLAN

Planning and Land Use Administration

Manages Planning Board filings, subdivisions, site plans, and zoning amendments. Keeps statutory deadlines visible and decisions documented.

Core Operations & Governance

FIX

Facilities and Infrastructure Exchange

Centralizes maintenance, inspections, and repairs. Connects facility requests to budgets and keeps capital projects aligned with long-term planning.

PAY

Payroll, Accruals, Year-End

Standardizes timesheets, accrual balances, payroll certifications, and rollovers. Builds staff trust with clarity and consistency.

CAPS

Capital Asset Planning and Stewardship

Connects capital requests, replacement timelines, and debt schedules. Gives boards and finance committees a long-term view of stewardship.

BUDGET

Budget, Unified Data

Unifies department submissions, forecasting, and variance monitoring. Produces consistent materials and transparent budget books for staff, boards, and residents.

AGENDA

Agendas, Governance, Engagement, Notices, Decisions, Access

Creates a clear framework for agenda preparation, statutory posting, and resident access. Ensures notices are timely, accurate, and traceable.

HEALTH

Health Enforcement, Applications, Licensing, Tracking, Hearings

Supports Boards of Health in permitting, inspections, licensing, and hearings. Maintains accurate records of public health enforcement.

PARK

Parks Administration, Recreation, Knowledge

Coordinates park reservations, programming, and facility use. Provides clarity for residents and efficiency for staff.

RETENTION

Records, Evidence, Transparency, Enforcement, Tracking, Integrity, Oversight, Navigation

Provides a defensible records retention system. Ensures records are classified, held, or released according to law and policy.

Named in Full, Built for Reality

Each module is named in full, paired with a clear acronym, and grounded in compliance. The names are plain and deliberate — so they can be read in a public meeting, survive audits, and remain clear through staff transitions.
