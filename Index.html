About PublicLogic

Nathan Boudreau, MPA, MCPPO is the Town Administrator of Hubbardston, MA and the founder of PublicLogic™. He’s a practitioner-builder who aligns policy, procurement, and day-to-day workflows so staff can serve residents with less risk and fewer clicks.

PublicLogic™ is a practitioner-led firm that delivers modernization frameworks for towns. We build compliance-first strategies and prototypes that meet Massachusetts law and keep municipal ownership in the driver’s seat.

Case Studies

HubbCONNECT (Hubbardston, MA)

Proof-of-concept digital government platform (~30% live) on CivicOptimize + Power Automate + Microsoft 365.

Challenge

  • Residents couldn’t see status across departments.
  • Staff time lost to duplicate entry.

Approach

  • Mapped policies and retention to state schedules.
  • Built vendor-friendly flows with town-owned logic.

Outcome

  • Board-ready exports and PRR-friendly logs.
  • Fewer clicks for staff; clearer answers for residents.

What we learned: start small, ship fast, and align to audits early.

FAQ

Do we need to switch vendors?

No. We design around your current stack and make vendors successful while the town keeps ownership and control.

How do you protect staff comfort?

Plain-language SOPs, fewer steps, and clear guardrails. Staff see what to do and when—without guessing.

How do VAULT™/ARCHIEVE™ work?

High-level only here: VAULT coordinates routing/retention; ARCHIEVE enforces rules and logs. Implementation details remain proprietary.

Are you Massachusetts-compliant?

Yes. We align to OML (M.G.L. c.30A), PRR (c.66 §10), Licensing (c.140/c.138), Building (c.143), Roads (c.40 §§6C–6D), 201 CMR 17.00, ADA, and state retention schedules.

Will residents understand it?

Yes. We use plain language, clear status, and mobile-first forms. Accessibility and translation are planned from day one.

Who owns the data and logic?

Your town. We build frameworks that keep control local and portable.

How do we start?

Book a short discovery call. We’ll map a 60–90 day pilot and align it to funding pathways.

What about privacy?

No tracking cookies by default. Minimal JS. Clear retention and access controls.
