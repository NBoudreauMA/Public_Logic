Town Clerk Portal – Workflow Guide

Use this portal to submit common clerk requests with conditional logic, digital certification and automatic routing. It combines multiple paper forms into a single, unified application maintained by the Town of Sutton. The services covered include business certificates (DBA), public records requests, meeting postings, general clerk inquiries, board & committee applications, dog license applications, and vital records requests. Please note: voter registration is managed by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, pole hearings are handled by the Department of Public Works, and swearing‑in appointments are still scheduled manually. If you prefer a paper form for dog licensing or vital records, you can download the dog license PDF application or use the vital records request form.

Routing & Approvals

Requests are routed automatically based on type. Each step supports approvals, rejections, requests for more information, comments and PDF generation.

Request Type Workflow
Business Certificate Clerk → Board of Health (if needed) → Assessor → Clerk Final Sign-Off
Public Records Clerk → Relevant Dept Head (if applicable) → Clerk Response & Archive
Meeting Posting Clerk Review → Timestamp → Optional Admin Notification
General Request Clerk Intake → Manually Assigned (if needed)
Board & Committee Application Clerk Intake → Select Board or Appointing Authority Review
Dog License Application Clerk Review → Issue License & Tag
Vital Records Request Clerk Intake → Verify eligibility → Produce Certificate & Mail
