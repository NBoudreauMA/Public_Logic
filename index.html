The PublicLogic Framework

VAULT™ governs routing, retention, transparency, and auditability across modules. ARCHIEVE™ enforces rules and holds based on state retention schedules. High-level only Mechanics are proprietary.

How vendors plug in without lock-in
PublicLogic defines the process & compliance layer; vendors (e.g., Polimorphic, CivicOptimize) provide the UI. Towns keep IDs, records, and audit logs.
